Top Patent Inventors

This Pareto chart represents the cumulative number of patents attributed to the top inventors. The x-axis lists the names of inventors, while the left y-axis shows the cumulative count of patents. The red line indicates the cumulative percentage of patents held by these inventors, demonstrating the distribution and concentration of patent ownership among leading inventors.

Patent Filings Over Time

This graph illustrates the number of patents filed each year from 1925 to 2025. The data shows a significant increase in patent filings, particularly from the mid-1980s onwards, indicating a growing trend in innovation and intellectual property protection.

Patent Publication Dates Over Time

This graph displays the number of patents published each year from 1925 to 2025. Similar to the filing date trends, there is a noticeable increase in published patents starting from the 1980s, reflecting an upward trend in the publication and dissemination of patent information.

Distribution of Patent Languages

: This pie chart shows the distribution of patents by language, with a total count of 3.5K patents. Each color represents a different language.

Common Keywords in Patents

This word cloud displays the most frequent keywords in patents related to sustainable aviation fuel. Larger words indicate higher frequency.

Time Between Patent Filing and Publication

This histogram shows the frequency of days between patent filing and publication. Most patents are published within 1000 days of filing.

Boxplot of Days Between Filing and Publication

This boxplot illustrates the distribution of days between patent filing and publication. It highlights the median, quartiles, and potential outliers.

Document Types by Top 10 Assignees

This bar chart displays the number of documents held by the top 10 assignees, categorized by document type (DE, EP, JP, US, WIPO).

Number of Documents per Top 10 Inventors

This bar chart shows the number of patent documents attributed to the top 10 inventors, indicating the most prolific contributors in the dataset.

Documents per Assignee and Inventor

This 3D plot illustrates the number of documents associated with different assignees and inventors. The x-axis represents assignees, the y-axis represents inventors, and the z-axis shows the number of documents. Each point corresponds to an assignee-inventor pair, with height indicating document count. The graph highlights the most prolific assignees and inventors, revealing key contributors and collaboration patterns in patent filings for sustainable aviation fuel.

Number of Documents by Publication and Filing Year

The color gradient indicates the document count, with darker colors representing fewer documents and brighter colors representing more documents. Users can interact with the plot by rotating and zooming to explore the relationship between filing and publication years, and the number of documents over time.
